about us

Heya! We're TurtleBlaze, and we make games.

We're 2 dutch geeks that use their collective nerd powers to create epic games. We've both been active in the gamejamming scene for quite a while now. Which is great fun, but we feel the time for experimenting is over.

Time to wake up.

Richard Lems

richard@turtleblaze.com

@rilemtwit


More often then not, biographies of people who make games start with a short story about their childhood.

Well my childhood was filled with Mario, Sonic, Gameboy, Anime, Comics, the list goes on. These have all shaped me as a creative individual. As a child I fell in love with games, I had to find out how to make these magical experiences myself.

It goes without saying that making games is a big part of my life now. It's something I've been doing for a long time, and I'll keep doing it as long as I can. :)
